Programme Booklet Available

We have prepared a booklet containing abstracts and details of the programme. This will not be available in printed form at the conference, so please print it out and bring it along with you. It is available here.

Poster Sizes

Each poster session will contain about 45 posters. There will be 24 panels of approximately 1.5m width and 1m height. There will be a poster on each side of these panels. That means that A0 landscape will be a good fit, but A0 portraint would be a little too big.

Colocated Events

Post Meeting Workshop: After AISTATS there will be a workshop on Active Learning and Experimental Design.
